We at Jai Utsav want to build a world where our seniors live an active and enthusiastic life. Without borders. Where sparks are reignited and moments missed earler are relived.

JaiUtsav is the first of its kind Age reversal facility, rejuvenating life for seniors at its facilities specially designed to meet the needs of aging. JaiUtsav means to win over thy self, we would love to erase the scars of time and the wrinkles of long years of neglect. Our loving healthcare professionals will ensure that cheeks turn pinker and smile broader. Soothes and happiness from within radiates among the residents. Working on auditory and visual stimulation motor skills, consistent scheduled meal times, bedtime’s medication, and vital parameters in seniors’ lives. Meditation, yoga, plants, music, reading, drawing, and painting play a pivotal role in calming the senses. We at JaiUtsav will develop an active connection with families and dear ones ensure kindness, and self-compassion towards every senior citizen and client.

II. HOW THE JOURNEY BEGAN AT JAIUTSAV

I learned Punjabi because it was the only language my Grandma knew. And she loved me beyond words can describe. The taste of her haath ke paranthe is still fresh in my mouth. So Daadi came to Bombay, an epitome of fitness she had never fallen ill, not once in 70 years. She had a quick cure for everything. But one fine morning we saw something amiss, and the Psychiatrist asked us “ke Hawa paani bada lijiye” But there was no improvement. We could not find any Dementia care centres anywhere in the country. Finally, after a bad bout of dementia, she passed away 5 years hence. Then my eldest Tau was diagnosed with Dementia. We tried again, albeit unsuccessfully. When our last Tau Ji also was diagnosed, I resolved to do something about it whenever I was capable enough.

Today I find myself capable to help my Daadi and Tau's even though they carry a different name.

My Daadi Still lives in My Heart

Thus, JaiUtsav Was Born.

We have an award-winning psychologist and loving staff who will ensure Dementia doesn't defeat the love of children.
