JaiUtsav Means

स्वयं पर जीत का उत्सव है, जय उत्सव।


  • “JaiUtsav means Self Victory. Victory over Thought, word and deed.” . Having emerged victorious in their past we welcome seniors to spend a great future with us.

    • Medicines - Lifestyle modifications

  • Medicines are important for a dementia patient, but they are just a part of the jigsaw puzzle. They need a complete care ecosystem, Lifestyle modification that revolves around a psycho- dynamic roadmap, Brain Stimulation activities, games and social support for the patient which go step by step in ensuring a fuller life. Various therapies may aid in brain jogging of your loved one and act as a catalyst for cognitive capabilities, also be supportive in enjoyment and illuminating lives. We at Jaiutsav ensure and try to improve their (quality of life function in daily activities. enhance cognition, mood and behaviour) overall experience of life. This, therefore, becomes challenging for families to offer appropriate quality time and remedial treatment to the Dementia/ Alzheimer patients at home. Thus JaiUtsav was innate. We at Jaiutsav, assure best-in-class Therapeutic approach designed by a panel of professionals that are suitable for your loved ones based on their medical conditions and history.

    • When to reach at Jaiutsav team

  • When you find your loved one’s showing more than one indicators on daily basis as mentioned below

  • Memory loss
  • Impaired Judgement
  • Difficulty performing familiar tasks and Abstract thinking
  • Faulty reasoning and Inappropriate behaviour
  • Problem with language (Confuse or difficult to express words)
  • Disorientation to time and Place
  • GIAT, Motor and Balance problem
  • Neglect of Personal Care and safety
  • Hallucination. Paranoia and Agitation

    RISK FACTOR DEMENTIA


RISK FACTOR FOR DEMENTIA

  • 1. AGE , SEX and Genetic
  • 2. Over Weight, Drug/ Smoking/ Alcohol
  • 3. Poor Education , Low physical Activity / Poor Nutrition
  • 4. Blood pressure. Blood Sugar, Cholesterol
  • 5. Depression/ Stress
  • 6. Heart Disease, Diabetes ,Vascular disease
  • 7. Brain Injury, Inflammatory stress/Tumour
  • 8. HIV Infection. Some Immunological Diseases

Management or Therapeutic Approach


  • Activities of daily living
  • Behavioural Control
  • Cognitive Remediation
  • Ant dementia Drugs

Therapeutic Approach/ Intervention made by Professional / Clinical psychologist


  • 1. PSYCHOSOCIAL INTERVENTION: Psychotherapy, Talk Therapy, CST (Cognitive stimulation therapy and training).
  • 2. Emotional Well-being Therapy: Reminisce therapy. ADL (activities of daily Living) Skill Training, Activity planning.
  • 3. Behavioural Therapy: Aroma Therapy. Music therapy, Sound therapy and ART therapy
  • 4. Occupational Therapy: Physical therapy, Lessening the burden on caretakers and Reducing the overall amount of care needed
  • 5. Multi-sensory Stimulation Therapy
  • 6. Validation and Reality Orientation therapy
  • 7. Pharmacological treatment
  • Memory aids such as calendars, diaries, schedules of daily routine, memory books, electronic device or Colour coding are used at our Centre.
